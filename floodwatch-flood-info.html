Stay Safe During Floods

Essential safety guidelines to protect yourself and your family during flood emergencies in Sri Lanka.

Before a Flood

  • Create an emergency kit with essentials
  • Know your evacuation routes
  • Keep important documents waterproof
  • Subscribe to FloodWatch alerts

During a Flood

  • Never walk through moving water
  • Don't drive through flooded areas
  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Stay away from electrical equipment

After a Flood

  • Return home only when safe
  • Document damage with photos
  • Clean and disinfect everything
  • Contact insurance company

Never Do This

  • • Walk or drive through flood water
  • • Touch electrical equipment when wet
  • • Ignore evacuation orders
  • • Return home before official clearance

Remember

  • • 15cm of moving water can knock you down
  • • 60cm of water can float a car
  • • Flood water contains contaminants
  • • Roads may be damaged beneath water
